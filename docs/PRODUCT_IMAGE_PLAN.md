## Photo Style

- consistent beige/paper background
- product centered
- soft natural shadows
- one clean packshot per product
- one lifestyle/ritual shot per product
- one detail/macro shot per product
- one comparison/scale shot where quantity matters

## Dimensions

- product card: 1200x1200
- product hero: 1600x1200
- social/story crop: 1080x1350

## Future AI / Creative Brief Prompts

Use these as creative briefs, not final legal copy:

- `Clean product packshot of [product], centered on warm beige paper, soft window light, subtle natural shadow, premium but grounded ritual shop aesthetic, no smoke, no people, square crop.`
- `Lifestyle ritual scene for [product], warm paper background, tidy tabletop, ceramic cup or setup accessories where appropriate, calm adult mood, no medical implication, no smoke, natural light.`
- `Macro detail photo of [product detail], warm neutral background, shallow depth of field, soft shadows, premium craft texture, no claims or text overlays.`
- `Comparison scale image showing [product A] beside [product B], warm beige paper background, clear size/quantity difference, clean ecommerce composition.`
