# Legal And CBD Copy Rules

This document is product and copy guidance, not legal advice. Final production copy should be reviewed before launch.

## Core CBD Rule

CBD vršički are sold and communicated as:

```text
CBD čaj / posušeni konopljini vršički za čaj
```

Do not write that CBD is for smoking.

## Allowed CBD Positioning

Allowed:

- CBD čaj
- posušeni konopljini vršički za čaj
- ritual z manj THC
- bolj jasna glava
- več kontrole
- mirnejši ritual
- večerni čaj
- za tiste dni, ko želiš ritual ohraniti, ampak ga narediti mirnejšega

Example wording:

```text
CBD čaj je za tiste dni, ko želiš ritual ohraniti, ampak ga narediti mirnejšega in bolj pod kontrolo.
```

```text
Posušeni konopljini vršički za čaj, ko želiš manj THC in bolj jasno glavo.
```

```text
Tvoj ritual, samo z drugo mero.
```

## Not Allowed CBD Claims

Do not claim or imply:

- CBD treats a disease
- CBD cures a condition
- CBD reduces anxiety as a medical claim
- CBD helps depression
- CBD helps insomnia
- CBD relieves pain
- CBD is anti-inflammatory
- CBD is therapeutic
- CBD has guaranteed effects
- CBD is safe for everyone
- CBD is for smoking
- CBD is for inhalation
- CBD replaces medicine

Avoid medical verbs:

- zdravi
- pozdravi
- lajša
- pomaga pri bolezni
- terapija
- protibolečinsko
- protivnetno
- proti anksioznosti
- proti nespečnosti

## Natural Human Copy

The wording should be human, not sterile legal language.

Good direction:

```text
Za mirnejši ritual, ko ne rabiš močnega THC momenta.
```

```text
CBD čaj za več kontrole nad večerom.
```

```text
Ko želiš obdržati ritual, ampak spustiti tempo.
```

Bad direction:

```text
Ta izdelek zdravi tesnobo.
```

```text
Primerno za kajenje.
```

```text
Naravna terapija za spanje.
```

## Membership Copy Rules

Use:

- `Član Zvij.si`
- `Zvij koda`
- `dobroimetje`
- `povabi prijatelja`
- `prijatelj dobi ugodnost`
- `ti dobiš dobroimetje`

Do not use:

- dealer program
- MLM
- preprodaja
- preprodajalec
- zaslužek
- provizija
- cash payout
- pasivni dohodek
- mreža prodajalcev

Allowed membership copy:

```text
Postani Član Zvij.si, deli svojo Zvij kodo in zbiraj dobroimetje za naslednji refill.
```

```text
Ko prijatelj uporabi tvojo Zvij kodo, oba dobita nekaj za naslednji setup.
```

Not allowed membership copy:

```text
Postani preprodajalec.
```

```text
Zasluži s svojo mrežo.
```

```text
Dealer program za tvojo ekipo.
```

## Brand Safety Rules

Do:

- speak to adults
- keep claims practical
- talk about ritual and setup
- avoid moral judgment
- avoid shock value
- keep CBD tea language consistent

Do not:

- glamorize illegal use
- target minors
- use youth-coded language
- make health promises
- imply intoxication from CBD tea
- imply resale or distribution by members

## Review Checklist

Before publishing CBD or membership copy, check:

- Does CBD copy say tea / dried hemp flower for tea?
- Does it avoid smoking/inhalation language?
- Does it avoid medical claims?
- Does it avoid guaranteed effects?
- Does membership copy use `Član Zvij.si`, `Zvij koda`, and `dobroimetje`?
- Does membership copy avoid resale, MLM, dealer, and cash-payout framing?
- Does the tone feel adult, calm, direct, and from within the culture?
