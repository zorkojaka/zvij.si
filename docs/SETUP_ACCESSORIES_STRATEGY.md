# Setup Accessories Strategy

Status: dev strategy draft.

## Purpose

Setup dodatki are small objects that complete the Zvij.si setup without turning the shop into a random accessories catalog.

The first accessory direction is lighters. A lighter is an "always with you" object, so it belongs naturally next to setup culture, membership, QR flows, and the line:

```text
Zvijače za zvijače.
```

The tone should stay adult, useful, and a little inside-culture. Do not make the lighter program childish, cheap, or novelty-only.

## Product Tiers

### Rezervni vžigalnik

- Role: backup / when needed / package add-on
- Price target: 1.50-2.50 EUR
- Branding: Zvij.si sticker or QR/member card in the package
- Avoid custom print for now

Use as a small practical add-on, not as a hero product.

### Clipper standard

- Role: main normal lighter
- Price target: 2.90-4.20 EUR
- Branding: use original Clipper identity first
- Add Zvij.si QR/member card in the package

This is the normal setup lighter. It should feel familiar and practical.

### Premium Clipper

- Role: premium personal setup object / gift / pride item
- Price target: 14.90-29.90 EUR
- Branding: premium packaging/card
- Do not put a cheap sticker directly on the premium lighter
- Possible future direction: Clipper x Zvij.si limited series

Premium Clipper should feel like an object someone keeps, not a disposable add-on.

## Shop Prototype Rules

- Category: `Setup dodatki`
- Products stay draft until supplier, prices, images, and packaging are confirmed.
- Do not configure payments.
- Do not imply confirmed Clipper collaboration before it exists.
- Do not overbrand cheap items.
- Do not make premium items feel like a stickered commodity.

## Homepage Copy

Use:

```text
Setup dodatki
Od rezervnega vžigalnika do premium Clipperja — majhne stvari, ki jih imaš vedno pri sebi.
```

## Open Decisions

- Supplier for each lighter tier.
- Whether standard Clipper variants are random, selected, or curated.
- Whether Premium Clipper is bought, engraved, boxed, or reserved for future limited series.
- Exact prices and margins.
- Product photos and packaging design.
- Whether accessories earn dobroimetje.
